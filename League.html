League Of Legends

What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a free to play online game that has a very large following.

The developer of this game is a company called Riot Games.

The game is constantly being changed and updated to balance aspects of the game and keep it interesting.

It is part of a genre of games called MOBAs or Multiplayer Online Battle Arena.

It is also one of the games that helped create the current popularity of ESports.

Riot Games holds its own regional and world wide tournaments for ESports.
