What is Bowling

For those that don't know, bowling is a great sport.

It is easy for people of any age to just go and have a good time playing

On the surface bowling probably seems like it is an easy sport.

However it is more complex than what a lot of people might think.

There are many different factures that can change the outcome when you roll your ball.

Your equipment, the conditions of the lane, the temperature/environment of the bowling alley,

and many more things can affect how a bowling ball will react when thrown.

It also requires a lot of practice in order to form a consistent and accurate throw.

Bowling has a profession league called the PBA (Professional Bowlers Association).

It hosts a lot of tournaments for players from all over the world to bowl.
